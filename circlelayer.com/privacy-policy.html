Privacy Policy

At Circle Layer, your privacy and data sovereignty are fundamental. As a decentralized platform, we are committed to minimizing data collection and giving users full control over their information.

1. Information We Collect

Circle Layer is built with privacy in mind. We collect only the information you willingly provide:

  • Basic Contact Information (e.g., name and email submitted through forms such as hackathon registration)
  • Voluntary Project Submissions ("What will you build")
  • Cookies limited to improving core site functionality and user experience

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information you share is used strictly for platform-related purposes:

  • Facilitating community participation and hackathon engagement
  • Sending essential communications (when opted in)
  • Improving site performance and UI responsiveness

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or monetize your personal data. Data may only be shared with:

  • Trusted service tools used for email or event management (e.g., form processors)
  • Legal authorities only when required by applicable law

4. Security

While we avoid storing sensitive information, we apply standard encryption and security practices to protect any minimal data we handle. However, in line with decentralized principles, we encourage users to avoid sharing unnecessary personal data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Circle Layer uses minimal cookies primarily for basic analytics and improving site performance. We do not use invasive tracking or third-party ad networks. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links

Some pages may link to third-party platforms (e.g., GitHub, event forms). We are not responsible for the data practices of external sites, so please review their privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may request to view, edit, or delete any personal data submitted through forms by contacting us at [email protected].

8. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this policy to align with evolving Web3 standards. Updates will be posted here with the latest effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or data-related requests, please reach out to us at [email protected].
